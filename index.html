About Us

Trusted Hands, Timeless Care

Al Rooliya Trading Establishment is a respected name in automotive care located in the heart of Qurayyat, Oman. With more than 27 years of serving our local community, we've built our reputation on honesty, skilled craftsmanship, and genuine care for our customers.

We know firsthand the specific challenges vehicles face on Oman's roads and in its climate, so our experienced mechanics give each car the personal attention it deserves.
